Introduction

Wow. You've downloaded Ninde! Firstly, thank you, and secondly, I hope she doesn't disappoint. If she does, please tell me why. I aim to please. You're in for darkness, deception, deviance, and maybe, if you're lucky some romance along the way. And that's just in the readme. Ninde is something fresh, and (I hope) interesting. She'll add a touch of withering wit, a pinch of attitude and a whole bucketful of naughtiness. So enjoy.

Ninde was a labour of love. A huge thank you to everyone who has helped along the way, a detailed list of whom can be found in this readme. I think I should give special thanks to K'aeloree, without whom this mod would never ever have got anywhere, and who is the best thing from Australia ever (sorry Danii Minogue), and who deserves all the credit for this. Or most of it.

Version 2 includes the romance, a friendship for female PCs, NPC banters, PC-initiated flirts, a chat menu and Ninde's interjections and ToB content.

Installation

PC

Ninde is a WeiDU mod, packaged in an extractable .RAR archive. Simply unrar "ninde_v2.rar" (WinRAR) into your Baldur's Gate II directory and run "setup-ninde.exe".

To uninstall, run "setup-ninde.exe" again and select the uninstall option.

Compatibility and Installation Order

The Ninde NPC mod should be compatible with all WeiDU mods, however, we cannot test every single one. It is only compatible with BGII: ToB. If you do encounter an error, please contact K'aeloree (kae@spellholdstudios.net) or alternatively post on the forums.

Although it is not required for Ninde to function properly, it is always a good idea install the latest version of the BG2 Fixpack.

Romance

Ninde will romance a male protagonist of any race or alignment with 13+ charisma points. The romance should begin shortly after arriving in Brynnlaw if these conditions are met, and lovetalks will occur in Spellhold and the Underdark. Ninde will be abducted, as per usual, but will NOT be vampirised - you should be able to find her easily enough.

If you haven't had a lovetalk for a little while, try moving on to another area. There are a few instances where the romance won't progress until you've reached a certain point.

,A note on the romance: Ninde isn't as prissy as some of your romance options. She won't end the relationship just because you say the wrong thing - in fact, she rather likes a man who'll stand up for himself. The choices that will end the romance are fairly obvious - as long as you let Ninde say her piece, and aren't too horrible to her, you should be alright.

Flirts: Flirting should be available as soon as you get Ninde in your party, if you meet the romance conditions. She won't take kindly to certain behaviors, however. More flirts should appear as the romance progresses.
